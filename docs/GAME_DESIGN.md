# Game design: Aetherwake

## Player fantasy

You are a Wayfinder: a field mage who restores a world where the old magical currents have ruptured. Magic is tactile and environmental. A tide spell makes water, ember lights and burns, stone creates traversal, and veil reveals what the eye cannot see.

## The Veiled Reach vertical slice

1. Enter the rain-soaked **Hollowmere Basin** and meet the displaced researcher Ilyra.
2. Learn Ember Lance from a collapsed forge shrine; burn a bramble gate.
3. Cross the flooded lowland with Tidal Bind, then uncover a hidden path using Veil Sight.
4. Use Stone Lift to reach the observatory and defeat the Thorn Warden with a party.
5. Cleanse the observatory heart, earn a permanent spell aspect, and unlock the next region.

## Combat and world rules

- Players equip four spells, each with mana, cooldown, elemental tags, and a world interaction.
- Enemies telegraph attacks and have readable stagger, resistance, and weak-point states.
- Two elements can create a short-lived reaction: ember + tide = scald cloud; tide + stone = rootable mud; veil exposes reaction opportunities.
- World changes are bounded, persistent per session, and replicated by stable prop IDs.

## Scope guardrails

The first public demo is one biome, four spells, three enemies, one boss, one co-op activity, and one 20–30 minute progression loop. Do not build a continent before this loop is fun, accessible, and stable online.
